Marvin JS : Calculate stereo

 Calculate stereo

Assigns the R or S label to a chiral center and the E or Z label to a double bond. The R/S and E/Z labels are determined via web service.

in Marvin JS both Optical isomerism and Geometric isomers can be determined with this tool.
