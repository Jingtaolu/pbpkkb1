Marvin JS : Navigate on the canvas

Zoom

  • Zoom In

Click Zoom in  button on the General toolbar and click on it as many time it is necessary. 

  • Zoom Out

Click Zoom Out button on the General toolbar and click on it as many time it is necessary.

  • Zoom In/Out

Scroll the mouse wheel over the canvas.

  • Zoom All

Click Zoom All  button on the General toolbar to optimize the size of the structure to the canvas size.

  • Zoom to Selection

Click Zoom to Selection button on the General toolbar to display only the selected structure on the canvas.

Scroll bars

Appears when its needed: when the structure is bigger than the canvas.
