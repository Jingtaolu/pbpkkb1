Marvin JS : Group Generics dialog

In Group Generics dialog you can choose the desired generic group from a predefined list of group generics (Beilstein or Reaxys generics).

How to draw a group generic

  • Open the  Group Generics dialog with the Reaxys Group Generics button on the Atom Toolbar.
  • After clicking the appropriate button, the dialog automatically closes, and the generic group will appear on the tip of the mouse cursor.
