Marvin JS : Delete

 Delete

Erases structures from the canvas.


How to delete a structure
Choose the Delete tool and drag diagonally, until a red highlight appears on the structure you want to erase. When you release the left mouse button, the highlighted parts will be deleted.

 

 

You can erase an atom or a bond by selecting the Delete tool and clicking on it.

 



Delete a structure with Rectangle or Freehand selection tool
Make a selection, then delete it by clicking on the Delete tool or pressing the Delete button on the keyboard.

 

 

In case of deleting a bond, its atoms will not disappear from the canvas. On the other hand, if you erase an atom, its bonds are also deleted.
