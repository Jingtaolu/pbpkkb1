Marvin JS : Charge

 Increase Charge

Successively increases the charge of an atom (ion, etc. ) with one unit (i.e. adds a positive charge to the atom).

 Decrease Charge

Decreases the charge of an atom with one unit for each clicking (i.e. adds a negative charge to the atom).
