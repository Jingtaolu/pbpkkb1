MarvinSketch : Move / Rotate / Zoom/ Scale

Move

    • An atom

Drag it with the mouse in Selection mode .

    • A selection
      1. Press Space-bar 1 time or move the mouse pointer toward the center until a blue rectangle appears and drag
      2. Click the center of the object and drag.

Rotate

    • A selection
      1. Press Space-bar 2 times or move the mouse pointer over the selection until a blue rotation symbol appears and drag.
      2. Click the center of the object and drag.
    • A selection in 3D
      1. Select Edit > Transform > Rotate in 3D  > ... and drag. 
      2. To change the view angle in 3D, press F7 (or the View > Mouse Mode > Rotate in 3D  menu item ) and drag.

 

Zoom

Set the magnification of the molecule on the canvas by the Zoom buttons.

If you have a mouse with a wheel, hold down the Ctrl key, and then scroll the wheel to zoom in or out. When a molecule is loaded into the editor, it is scaled automatically to fit the window.


    • Zoom In:

Click Zoom In  on the General toolbar and click on it as many time it is necessary  or

Select a level from the Zoom tool's drop-down list (or from the View > Zoom Level menu) or to All. (The All option zooms in or zooms out the objects to the full size of the canvas.)

    • Zoom Out:

Click Zoom Out  on the General toolbar and click on it as many time it is necessary orm

Select a level from the Zoom tool's drop-down list (or from the View > Zoom Level menu) and set the magnification to a smaller value..

    • Zoom In/Out:

Press F6 and drag vertically to zoom.

Scroll the mouse wheel over the canvas while pressing the Ctrl key.
