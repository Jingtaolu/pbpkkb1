MarvinSketch : Geometric transformation of structures and objects

Geometric transformation functions (Move, Rotate, Flip, Mirror, Invert) can be used from the Edit > Transform menu option on the whole molecule or only on the selected part of the structure.

  • Moving&Rotating
  • Scaling
  • Flip a molecule
  • Mirror a molecule
  • Central inversion of a molecule
