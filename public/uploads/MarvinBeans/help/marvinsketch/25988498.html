MarvinSketch : Marvin OLE User's Guide

Marvin OLE is the ChemAxon solution for Microsoft's Object Linking and Embedding (OLE) technology. It is supported by Windows operating systems. Marvin OLE offers an easy way to embed MarvinSketch drawings into other applications, like Microsoft Office Family products. Embedded Marvin Documents can be edited in-place with a fully functional Marvin Sketch.

Word Documents, Excel Sheets, and many other files containing embedded Marvin Documents are easily distributable. Even people who do not have Marvin and JChem_NET_API installed can access the content as an image, however, to edit the Marvin Document itself, they must have a properly installed Marvin with OLE support.
