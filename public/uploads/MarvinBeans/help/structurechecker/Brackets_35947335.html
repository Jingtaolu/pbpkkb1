Structure Checker : Brackets

  • Checker searches for brackets. Brackets are usually associated with generic S-groups.
  • Fixer offers removing brackets (removebrackets).


Example :

Brackets Checker (brackets) Warning: Fix
Remove Brackets (removebrackets)
Detect brackets

Note: Checker does not detect brackets denoting polymers and mixtures. Graphical brackets (brackets not related to S-groups) are not detected.
