Structure Checker : Structure Checker Concepts

The core idea of Structure Checker is the strict separation of the structure checking and fixing processes. This concept makes Structure Checker a highly customizable interactive structure manipulation tool.

The two-step Structure Checker process is carried out by:

  1. Checkers, that screen the chemical compounds for structural concerns.

  2. Fixers, that correct the reported structural problems.
